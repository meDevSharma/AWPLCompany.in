Addiction

Addiction is when someone feels a strong and ongoing urge to use a certain substance or keep doing a behavior, even if they know it's harming them or causing trouble in their life. It's more than just a bad habit — it's something that becomes hard to control and can affect health, relationships, and daily activities. People with addiction often find it difficult to stop, even when they really want to, because their brain and body get used to the feeling or reward that the habit brings.
