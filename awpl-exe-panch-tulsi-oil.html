Learn About Panch Tulsi

Panch Tulsi is a blend made from five varieties of tulsi, an herb deeply respected in Indian culture and Ayurvedic tradition. Together, these tulsi extracts work to support the immune system, promote healthy skin, and boost overall wellness for the body. The combination, often available as liquid "tulsi drops", is considered a powerful Ayurvedic remedy. If you're from India, you're probably already familiar with Tulsi — it's not just an ingredient but a sacred plant often worshipped in households for centuries. This "holy basil" has long been called a universal herb, prized for its healing properties. Blending five different types of tulsi into one potent extract enhances its effects even further, giving a boost to natural health and vitality.
